Maggie Paquette: Branding

Planning

I needed to consider all the designs and styles I like to ensure I made myself a brand that I can be proud of.

The design of the symbol for my logo is based off my initials signature, where the M and the P are attached. I kept this theme, but I made it cleaner and readable with the much respected typeface of Helvetica. I considered my bubbly and silly attitude, and thought that adding light colours and hues would best represent me.
