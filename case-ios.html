IOS: Coffee Time

App Definition

The need of the user is to satisfy their thirst, hunger and energy boost (that coffee would supply). Coffee fix would allow you to purchase your coffee ahead of time and have it ready when you pick it up.

Target Audience

The users of my product range from young adults to adults. They are always on the run, and they seem to be rushing everywhere to be on time. They are always busy between attending class, work, meetings etc. Of course they have needs like everyone else, they need food and drinks, and many of them do need their coffee.

Research

Apps on the Market

Starbucks:

Their app makes it easy and fast to pay for your drinks and food. You can order ahead of time and pick up your drinks without waiting in line. You can even tip your barista, in the process.

Grubhub:

Order food through this app. You can order both pickup and delivery. Enter your location and it will find the restaurants that are close to you. You can check out the menus, order, and even pay (with credit, paypal or cash) on this app. The special feature that this app allows for you id to search a specific food item and it will show you that food item that many different places might have

What Makes Coffee Time Different

The issue with apps on the market today is how bombarded they are with many features that have mediocre results. Starbuck app is only for their chain and no other coffee chains. My app would cover local coffee shops as well as multiple chain coffee shops.

Many of the pickup and delivery apps have many issues with the delivery part of the application. Deliveries do not always show up, or are extremely late and the food is cold by the time that they get there. My app would focus solely on the order being placed and prepared for the person to pick up.

Screens and Features

Home Screen

Shows you your different identities that you have stored on the application.

Add a New Identity

Adding the new identity can be as easy as taking a photo of your id. There is also a manual input where you put the card number and the database will manually fill out the rest of the information.

Identity Options

This pages shows the options of each identity. The about tab will have all the information about the card type and what it is used for. The history tab shows where and what date you used that identity.

View of Card use History

In the history tab you can select uses of your identity to read more about what you used that identity for. Such as your trip to the hospital where you can read about what happened in that card use.
