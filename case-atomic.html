Atomic Rooster

Planning

Atomic rooster needed a logo that matched their personality, and atmosphere. I created a moldboard that allowed for the look and feel of the interior and environment of the bar.

Considerations had to be made of what font to use. Knowing that a good round sans serif should be used to represent their closeness with the new generation. This lead me to use the font Avenir. The colour choices were used to work with the yellow of the atomic symbol, and the brown of rooster feathers.
