Geominimal: Typeface Design

About the font

Geominimal is a mono weight typeface designed to look futuristic. It is made revolving around a 10 point circle by adding small aspects to make it resemble the english alphabet letters. This typeface would be best used in a display setting because it is not easily readable at a smaller point sizes.
